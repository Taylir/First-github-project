The basic of basics

  • (color): Using color or background color it is very easy to change letter or back ground color.

    • (color: red)
    • (background-color: lightblue)
    • (background-color: lightblue); (max-width: 825px); Note: with out the width background would go to end of set width.

  • (Padding & Margin): The options that set how far away from edges of content they are. Padding is on inside and Margin is on the outside of the content. So as we see the margin moves the background and words down 20px from the previous bullet point. While the padding moves the words 20px down from the top of the background color.

    • Some background color with 20px top margin
